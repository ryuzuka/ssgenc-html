Eco-friendly Management

Shinsegae E&C is aware of its social responsibility for environmental issues.

Shinsegae E&C recognizes its social responsibility for environmental issues, complies with environmental laws and regulations, and plays a leading role in energy-saving and national environment conservation by actively utilizing eco-friendly construction elements.

Our employees related to the environment and all personnel who perform environmental tasks on our behalf shall familiarize themselves with the environment policy through education and training, establish an efficient management system and implement, document, maintain and continuously improve it by reflecting the requirements of the management system.

Air Pollutant Emission Control

At our current construction sites, we are proactively implementing environmental protection by minimizing the emission of air pollutants.
The surrounding areas are periodically cleaned with water, and our equipment operates with installed contaminant reduction devices.
In addition, continuous monitoring is carried out to control noise generated within the sites.

  • Air pollutant (fugitive dust) emission control
  • Use of equipment for exhaust gas reduction
  • Noise and fine dust management

Management of Water Quality and Soil Pollutants

A sedimentation facility is installed on construction sites to prevent soil leakage to the outside, and each site is inspected and measured for soil pollutants. Besides, we are managing so that pollutants inside the site do not go outside.

    Designed to reduce the thickness of the foundation by distributing the column load transferred to the foundation. Applied to Goyang Starfield, Daejeon Science Complex, and many other residential sites including Villiv Sky.

Composite Transfer Beam Method

We are scheduled to apply for Green Technology Certification for the Composite Transfer Beam engineering method.
This method can reduce the use of raw materials and the amount of waste and carbon dioxide by slimming down the supplementary materials by making the large reinforced concrete transfer beam structure, which is often applied in residential buildings, into a steel structure.

※ Eco-friendly features of Composite Transfer Beam
- Compared with the reinforced concrete transfer beams, the height and width of the beam can be reduced by slimming down the supplementary materials, which ultimately helps save the use of raw materials. Moreover, it shortens the construction period through factory manufacturing.
- When constructing the transfer beams, no need to install structural support, simplifying the on-site process and generating less waste.
