Safety & Health Management

We will implement sustainable management by putting safety first and fulfilling our social responsibilities.

All employees shall take on a challenging and evolving attitude to fulfill corporate social responsibilities by rigorously managing the goal achievement progress, innovating the business processes, and achieving ZERO in terms of disaster based on the safety and health management systems (KOSHA- MS), and support the company to achieve continuous development.

Safety Activities

01Activate the risk assessment system

We carry out preventive activities to establish and check safety measures on key management items by conducting risk assessments on a daily basis on risk factors for each work process.

02Safety inspection by the management

The entire management strives to achieve zero in terms of disasters at all work sites by conducting an on-site safety patrol at least once a month to identify risk factors and difficulties at worksites and provide support.

03Real-time safety management

We prevent the factors that cause disaster in advance by leveraging the mobile safety management system, which enables our company to communicate with our business partners on a real-time basis on risk factors in the worksites.

04Safety & Health Activities

We are striving to enroot a safe culture where all stakeholders can participate by establishing a system of communication and cooperation involving all our stakeholders including our employees, business partners, and workers.

  • Risk prevention education
  • PM Check
  • Machine Check
  • Partner meeting
  • Employer couseling
