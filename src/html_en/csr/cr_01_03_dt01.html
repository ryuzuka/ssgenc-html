Support the Complex Cultural Center for the Youth of Hanam City

2021.01.01

Remodeling of the Hope Toy Library is Shinsegae E&C’s main social contribution activity started in 2014 that best represents its company's characteristics. The program repairs old or outdated children's libraries and related facilities and donates toys and children's books based on the partnership with Child Fund Korea. The program helps give hopes children and was carried out for over 30 facilities in 24 regions across the nation including Seoul, Daejeon, Jeonju, Gwangju, Daegu, Ulsan and Jeju.
