Ombudsman

Please inform us of any actions such as corruption, unlawful acts, or unfair trade practices
that our employees may have been engaged in, which may be against our corporate social responsibility.

  • Your identity will strictly be kept confidential during the investigation.
  • The following will not be accepted for reporting.

    Simple complaints/inquiries, unclear contents, groundless slander, legal response between individuals, etc.

Please contact the customer service center if you have any questions related to customer service such as after-sales service, defects, and sales of apatments/shopping malls as ombudsman will not reply to such cases

Items accepted for reporting

  • 01 Unfair business handling and unfair trade practices by our employees
  • 02 Bribery, requests for entertainment, and violations to the CSR management
  • 03 Illegal acts and other problems that require improvement
