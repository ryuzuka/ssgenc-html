All employees of Shinsegae E&C are committed to enroot fair business culture through the implementation of the Compliance Program (CP).

What is CP?

The Fair Trade Compliance Program (CP/Compliance Program) is a voluntary compliance system to establish a fair market competition order by enabling a company to comply with the fair trade act while executing activities that prevent illegal actions. The Fair Trade Commission encourages companies to voluntarily introduce and spread the CP by offering programs to alleviate disciplinary actions imposed against them when illegal actions are detected.

8 Key Elements of CP

  • 01 Prepare and implement compliance standards and procedures
  • 02 CEO’s commitment to compliance and support
  • 03 Appointment of a compliance officer
  • 04 Development and usage of the compliance Manual
  • 05 Offering the CP training to employees
  • 06 Establishment of internal monitoring system that includes compliance monitoring, audit and etc
  • 07 Disciplinary actions imposed against employees violating the Fair Trade Act
  • 08 Efficiency evaluation and improvement measures
