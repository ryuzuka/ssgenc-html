4. Period of retention and use of personal information

The Company immediately destroys personal information once the purpose of collection and usage of such information has been fulfilled.
However, in cases where personal information is required to be retained by relevant laws and regulations, such information will be kept for the period required by those laws and regulations.

  • - In case where an investigation or inspection is in progress due to a violation of relevant laws and regulations, until the investigation or inspection is completed
  • - Records on consumer complaints or dispute handling (Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, etc.): 3 years
  • - Web browsing history or user tracking data (Protection of Communications Secrets Act): 3 months
5. Procedure and method of destruction of personal information

In principle, the Company, without delay, destroys personal information once the purpose of collection and usage of such information has been fulfilled. The procedure and method of destruction are as follows.

Destruction Procedure

Despite the retention period of personal information that has obtained consent from the users expires or the purpose has been fulfilled, if it is required by applicable laws and regulations, the Company will retain personal information. In such cases, personal information will be transferred to a separate database (DB) or stored in a different storage location. In addition, the personal information transferred to the DB will not be used for any other purpose other than retention unless otherwise required by law.

Destruction Method

Personal Information printed out on paper is destroyed by shredding using a document shredder, or incinerating it. Personal Information stored in electronic file format shall be deleted by technical means that would render the records unrecyclable.

6. Rights of users and legal representatives, and the method of its exercise

The Users may search or modify personal information provided or request deletion.
For those who wish to search or modify personal information, please contact our Personal Information Protection Officer in writing, phone, or e-mail for immediate action.
When the Users request to modify errors, the Company will not use or provide personal information until such request has been completed.
If incorrect personal information has been already provided to third parties, the Company will immediately notify the parties and request modification.
As for personal information terminated or deleted upon the Users’ request, the Company will process such personal information in accordance with the retention and usage period of collected personal information and ensure such information will not be queried or used for any other purposes.

8. Measures to secure the safety of personal information

The company is taking the following technical measures to ensure safety so that personal information is not lost, stolen, leaked, altered, or damaged in processing personal information of the data subject.

  • 1. Regular self-audit
    self-audit is conducted regularly to ensure stability related to personal information handling.
  • 2.Minimize the personal information handling staff and education
    Managing personal information measure has been implemented by designating the staff to handle personal information, and only the designated person is in charge of managing the personal information.
  • 3.Establish and implement an internal management plan
    For safe processing of personal information, an internal management plan has been established and implemented.
  • 4. Technical countermeasures against hacking
    To prevent personal information leakage and damage due to hacking or computer viruses, security programs are installed, updated and inspected periodically, and systems are installed in areas with controlled access from the outside, and technically/physically monitored and blocked.
  • 5. Encrypting Personal Information
    The password of the user is encrypted, stored, and managed, so only the user can know important data and uses separate security functions such as encrypting files and transmission data or using file locking functions.
  • 6. Safely store and prevent forgery of access records
    Access records are stored and managed for at least two years to prevent forgery and theft of access records. Security functions are used to prevent forgery, theft, or loss of access records.
  • 7. Restriction of access to personal information
    Access rights to database system that handles personal information is given, changed, and canceled to control access of personal information, and unauthorized access from the outside is regulated by using an intrusion prevention system.
  • 8.Use of a lock for document security
    Keep documents containing personal information, auxiliary storage media, etc. in a safe place with a lock.
  • 9. Access control for unauthorized persons
    There is a separate physical storage place where personal information is stored, and access control procedures are established and operated.
