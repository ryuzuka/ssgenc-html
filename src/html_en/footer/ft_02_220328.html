2. Purpose of collection and use of personal information

The Company processes personal information only for the following purposes, and personal information will not be used for any other purposes or provided to third parties. In addition, personal information is processed and used within the retention period agreed upon when collecting personal information required by laws or from users.

  • For inquires: Personal identification and identity verification, prevention of illegal use, and inquiry/complaint handling
  • For reporting: Receipt of report contents, fact check, information on handling and notification of results
3. Provision of personal information to third parties

The Company does not provide personal information of the Users to external parties. However, exceptions are made in the following cases:

  • - In case the Users have provided prior consent
  • - In case of statutory requirements, or there is a request from an investigatory agency in accordance with the procedures and methods stipulated by applicable laws and regulations for the purpose of investigation

5. Period of retention and use of personal information

The Company immediately destroys personal information once the purpose of collection and usage of such information has been fulfilled.
However, the following information will be retained for the specified period for the following cases:

  • For inquiries: Destroyed after 1 year from the date of completion of inquiry
  • For reporting: Destroyed after 1 year from the date of completion of reporting

However, in cases where personal information is required to be retained by relevant laws and regulations, such information will be kept for the period required by those laws and regulations.

  • - In case where an investigation or inspection is in progress due to a violation of relevant laws and regulations, until the investigation or inspection is completed
  • - Records on consumer complaints or dispute handling (Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, etc.): 3 years
  • - Web browsing history or user tracking data (Protection of Communications Secrets Act): 3 months
6. Procedure and method of destruction of personal information

In principle, the Company, without delay, destroys personal information once the purpose of collection and usage of such information has been fulfilled. The procedure and method of destruction are as follows.

Destruction Procedure

Despite the retention period of personal information that has obtained consent from the users expires or the purpose has been fulfilled, if it is required by applicable laws and regulations, the Company will retain personal information. In such cases, personal information will be transferred to a separate database (DB) or stored in a different storage location. In addition, the personal information transferred to the DB will not be used for any other purpose other than retention unless otherwise required by law.

Destruction Method

Personal Information printed out on paper is destroyed by shredding using a document shredder, or incinerating it. Personal Information stored in electronic file format shall be deleted by technical means that would render the records unrecyclable.

7. Rights of users and legal representatives, and the method of its exercise

The Users may search or modify personal information provided or request deletion.
For those who wish to search or modify personal information, please contact our Personal Information Protection Officer in writing, phone, or e-mail for immediate action.
When the Users request to modify errors, the Company will not use or provide personal information until such request has been completed.
If incorrect personal information has been already provided to third parties, the Company will immediately notify the parties and request modification.
As for personal information terminated or deleted upon the Users’ request, the Company will process such personal information in accordance with the retention and usage period of collected personal information and ensure such information will not be queried or used for any other purposes.

8. Matters concerning the installation, operation and rejection of devices that automatically collect personal information

The Company uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service tool provided by Google, to provide better services for the Users. Google Analytics collects behavioral information of the Users through cookies; only non-identifying information that cannot identify individual users is collected. Nevertheless, the Users may refuse to use Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Blocking Browser Add-on (https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) or refusing to set cookies on the web browser.

PC
  • From IE: [Tools Menu] > [Options] > [General] > [Web Browsing History] > Delete Cookies
  • From Chrome [Web Browsing History] > Delete web browsing history
Mobile
  • Android: [Settings] > [Google Settings] > [Advertising] > Activate Disable Personalized Ads
  • iPhone: [Settings] > [Privacy] > [Tracking] > Deactivate [Allows Apps to Request to Track]
9. Measures to secure the safety of personal information

The Company takes the following measures to ensure safety so that personal information is not lost, stolen, leaked, altered, or damaged when processing personal information of the Users:

  • - The Company strives to protect personal information by installing server and network firewall systems for the website.
  • - The Users’ personal information and password are encrypted, stored, and managed. The Users can only access such encrypted information. Other protection measures are implemented for further protection of important data, such as encrypting the relevant files or transmitted data or using locking functions, etc.
  • - Security programs are installed, regularly updated, and reviewed to prevent leakage and damage of personal information from hacking, computer viruses, and other factors. Systems are installed in restricted areas with limited access, which are technologically and physically monitored and blocked.
  • - Only administrators with internal authority can access personal information through the account, and we make every effort to enhance security awareness by providing continuous security training programs to administrators.
