Education system

  • - We regard talent development as the driving force for future growth and actively provide support through various in-house and external training programs to enable our talents to grow into professional construction engineers.
  • - The CDP program implemented company-wide is operated systematically to maximize the competency of our employees and our company.
  • New Employee Development
    • Basic training: Share the culture and vision of Shinsegae E&C and conduct basic job training for new employees, enabling them to acquire basic knowledge on the in-house systems and basic work knowledge
    • OJT: Support the new employees to acquire and familiarize with basic work knowledge through 1:1 mentoring training for 3 months after joining the company
  • Development of Key Talents
    • After selecting critical talents among junior employees, help them acquire the latest technology in the construction industry while receiving education for professional knowledge by linking them to external educational institutions
    • C-MBA: After selecting critical talents among senior-ranking employees, help them acquire the latest technology in the construction industry while receiving education for professional knowledge by linking them to external educational institutions
    • Preliminary education for construction managers: Foster “skilled next-generation construction managers” by offering education programs that enhance a construction manager's leadership and job competency
    • Customized education for executives: Provide external professional education that is relevant to one’s duties
  • Job Training
    • In-house job training: Provide competency training for each job such as construction/public service, mechanical engineering/electricity, safety management, etc.
    • External job training: Provide training through education and seminars offered by external educational institutions relevant to one’s job.
    • Support for obtaining job qualifications: Provide incentives for obtaining job qualifications, offer discounts for private institutions for technology, etc.
  • Other Education
    • Language: Offer online language course, foreign language education via phone or video, etc.
    • E-learning: Offer online education in various fields such as liberal arts, jobs, and language through PC or mobile
    • Provide on ethical management, fair trade, and human rights in addition to statutory education (including sexual harassment prevention education, etc.)
    • Education credit system: Make it mandatory for our employees to invest certain hours or more per year in education as a way of encouraging employees to develop their competency and to create a self-driven learning culture (make education practical by reflecting the credits obtained in the employee assessment system)
