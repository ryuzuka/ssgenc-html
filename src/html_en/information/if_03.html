BI

SHINSEGAE VILLIV
comes into being with a belief in various lifestyles

It realizes “Lifestylement” that goes beyond an apartment.
VILLIV reflects Shinsegae E&C's philosophy for “a good house and good life.”

Brand Vision

  • Delicate comfort A comfortable space carefully tailored to the speed of my life

    Offers a new level of convenience by moving the house from periphery to the center of my life. Shinsegae VILLIV offers physical convenience to one’s life and considers pleasure and comfort to all movements and gazes at every moment spent at home.

  • Refined pleasure An experience where everything in Shinsegae is connected around the house

    The uniqueness that comes from Shinsegae Group's synergy.
    With lifestyle housing services tailored with Shinsegae contents, living spaces and very details of one’s life are filled with psychological premium and pride stylishly set by Shinsegae.

  • Embracing the lifestyle A living space that flexibly embraces various lifestyles

    A 'personal home' that reflects the lifestyles of various people.
    As way of life became more diversified, Shinsegae VILLIV proposes a residential space suitable for various lifestyles and also offers new inspiration through exchanges of life.
